GDSC autumn of code is here!

It is the most extensive open-source program ever organized at IGDTUW, centered around learning opportunities for women in technology! Our aim is to inspire, challenge and motivate passionate innovators, especially women, to pursue their careers in Open Source and develop solutions to current world problems. We believe that together we can make a difference. Participants can work on numerous projects under our guidance. Our skilled mentors will help all the participants in nurturing and polishing their technical skills and contributing to various projects from the comfort of their homes over our fifty-day contribution period.
